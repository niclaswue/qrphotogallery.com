## What we store

- **Host accounts**: email address, password hash, chosen plan, sign-up
  language and country.
- **Galleries**: the gallery name, optional event date, design and settings
  selected by the host.
- **Guest uploads**: the original photos and videos guests submit, plus an
  optional guest name when the host enables that setting. Guests need no
  account. A functional cookie remembers that their browser has submitted.
- **Payments**: processed by Lemon Squeezy; we never see card details.

## Analytics

We use PostHog for anonymous product analytics. Session replay and
cross-page tracking only activate after cookie consent.

## Storage location and retention

Gallery files and application data are stored on infrastructure located in
the European Union. Each gallery has a 100 GB allowance and remains available
for one year from creation. After that period it is removed according to our
retention process. Hosts can delete a gallery and its uploaded originals
earlier from their dashboard.

We use service providers only where needed to operate the product, including
Lemon Squeezy for payment processing and, when analytics consent is given,
PostHog's EU service. Processing is governed by the applicable data-processing
agreements.

## Your rights

Contact us at hello@qrphotogallery.com to request access, correction,
restriction, portability or deletion of your data, or to object to processing.
You may also complain to your competent data-protection authority. You can
delete individual galleries and their uploaded files from your dashboard;
contact us if you want your account deleted as well.
