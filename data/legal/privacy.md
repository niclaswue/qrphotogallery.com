## What we store

- **Host accounts**: email address, password hash, chosen plan, sign-up
  language and country.
- **Events and prompts**: the content hosts type in.
- **Guest uploads**: the photos guests submit, plus an optional guest name
  when the host enables that setting. Guests need no account; we set
  functional cookies to remember upload progress on their device.
- **Payments**: processed by Lemon Squeezy; we never see card details.

## Analytics

We use PostHog for anonymous product analytics. Session replay and
cross-page tracking only activate after cookie consent.

## Your rights

Contact us at support@example.com to access or delete your data. Deleting
your account removes your events, prompts and all uploaded photos.
