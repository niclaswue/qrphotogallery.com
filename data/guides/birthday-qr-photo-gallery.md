## Every birthday photo in one gallery

At a birthday party, the best photos are the ones you never see — taken across the room, on someone else's phone, then lost in a group chat. A QR code photo gallery brings them all together. Print one code, guests scan and upload, and the surprise moment, the cake, the dancing and the candid table shots all land in one shared gallery in full quality.

It's a web page, not an app, so there's nothing to install and nothing to explain. That's what turns a passive room into everyone contributing photos.

## Why one QR code beats the usual scramble

- **No app, no login.** Every guest can join, from teenagers to grandparents.
- **Original quality.** No messaging-app compression — the photos stay good enough to print or frame.
- **One place, one download.** Everything collects in a single gallery you download as one ZIP.
- **Private by default.** Only people with the link or code can see the gallery.

## Set it up in minutes

1. **Create the gallery.** [Start here](%LP%/create) with the birthday name and date; your QR code is generated instantly.
2. **Put the code on the tables.** Add a card or a sign that says "Scan to share your photos."
3. **Mention it once.** A quick word when the cake comes out is all it takes.
4. **Download and keep it.** Save every original as a ZIP after the party.
