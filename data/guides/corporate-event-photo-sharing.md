## One gallery for the whole event

Conferences, team offsites, product launches and the company holiday party all produce great photos that never reach the people who could use them — scattered across attendees' phones and personal accounts. A QR code photo gallery pulls them into one shared place. Print the code for tables, lanyards and screens; attendees scan and upload; and your team walks away with every photo and video in original quality.

No app and no login means even the busiest attendee contributes in seconds — the difference between a handful of official shots and a full gallery of the day.

## Why teams use a QR code gallery

- **Effortless for attendees.** Scan, upload, done — nothing to install.
- **Original quality for reuse.** Full-resolution files you can use on the intranet, in recap emails and on social.
- **One organizer download.** Every file in a single ZIP, no chasing colleagues.
- **Private and controlled.** Visible only to people with the link; not indexed or public.
